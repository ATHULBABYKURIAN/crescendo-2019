About

CResCEndo is the annual inter-collegiate Technical Festival of Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering, Bandra which is platform that helps to showcase skills and originality through innovative events throughout the two days.


What's Happening This Year

Highlights

Amazing Cash Prizes

Total cash prizes worth more than Rs. 1,00,000/-

Exciting Events

Wide variety of technical and fun events to choose from.

Prestigious Trophies

Great Opportunities

Excellent platform provided for Technical Projects and Ideas
